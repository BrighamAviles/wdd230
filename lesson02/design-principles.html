White Space and Clean Design

https://www.airbnb.com/

Airbnb's website balances whitespace with just the right amount of content. The search functionality is given breathing room, while listing previews are cleanly organized into visual grids with lots of surrounding space.

PARC: Alignment

https://mailchimp.com/

The Mailchimp website has a clean, aligned design that makes excellent use of whitespace and grid-based layouts. Right from the homepage, you can see the content is structured into distinct sections with consistent alignment.
